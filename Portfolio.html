About Me

My name is Gerus Hays but, people just call me Gus for short. Welcome to my page!
I grew up all over the United States growing up in a military household and then joining the Army myself at the age of 18. I recently got out of the military December of 2021 after serving 8.5 years as a UAS Instructor Pilot flying the MQ-1C Gray Eagle. During my career, my interest in problem solving and learning how things worked grew exponentially.

I am now attending the Full Stack Software Development 6-month online bootcamp with University of Utah and learning a ton of information about what I have been striving towards accomplishing for years. Stay tuned to learn more about what I can accomplish in the realm of Software and computer science!
